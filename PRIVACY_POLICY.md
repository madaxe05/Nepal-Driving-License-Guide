**Last Updated: December 28, 2025**

## Introduction

Nepal Driving License Guide ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information We DO NOT Collect

This app is designed with your privacy in mind. We want to make it clear that:

- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
- **No User Accounts**: The app does not require registration or login
- **No Location Data**: We do not access or track your location
- **No Contact Information**: We do not access your contacts, phone numbers, or email addresses
- **No Analytics**: We do not use analytics services to track your usage
- **No Advertising**: We do not display advertisements or use advertising networks

### Information Automatically Collected

The app does not automatically collect any information about your device or usage patterns.

All content in the app is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

This data never leaves your device and is completely under your control.
